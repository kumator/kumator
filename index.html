From The Rector

Welcome! I formally welcome you to our great institution,FEDERAL POLYTECHNIC WANNUNE, which is the citadel of academic excellence. It is a Federal Government owned tertiary institution accredited by the Federal Ministry of Education (FME) and the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE) in Nigeria, West Africa.

The extent law establishing it assigns to the institution the task of providing well trained and highly skilled manpower for the National economy in the areas of Engineering technology, Science environmental studies, Management studies and Law. We have intensive entrepreneurship programme that prepares our student to compete in the global economy after graduation. We have the capacity and purpose to prepare our students for their chosen careers.

As an institution, it is our duty to help our students achieve their visions, build and exhibit good moral values, and eventually become good leaders for the future. We assure you that choosing this great institution for a vibrant career development will never be regrets.

The Polytechnic life consists of many people from diverse social communities.
